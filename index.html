ENGR 11A & Jonghyeon Lee

A Collection of Projects Undertaken by a Brandeis Student in the University's First Engineering Course, Intro to Design Methodology. Instructed by Ian Roy and Ben Rogers in Fall 2023.

Week 1 Project: Prusa Mini+

September 5th, 2023 and September 8th, 2023
Benchy
Workspace
Jonghyeon (on left) and Greg (on right)
Assembly Parts
❮ ❯

For Week 1 Project, I was introduced to 3D printing. With my partner Greg, we assembled a single extruder 3D printer Prusa Mini+. Then, we printed a universally recognized calibration test Benchy. Lastly, we explored various print setting to optimize the printing time.

Week 2 Project: Remix 3D Model

September 12th, 19th, and 21st

For the Week 2 Project, I explored CAD and CAM in greater detail by remixing 3D files. I imported some 3D models from online and combined them using Fusion 360 to create a new usage. Inspired by the American Express centurion army, I created a stand for their credit card, in which the army is standing on the logo and defending himself with the card.

Week 3 Project: Alarm Clock

September 29th, October 3rd, 6th, and 10th
❮ ❯

For Week 3 Project, I was introduced to soldering and creating a perfect enclosure for a soldered alarm clock. With my partner Danny, we assembled an alarm clock using the circuit board and electrical components. Then, we 3D printed an enclosure using Fusion 360.

Week 4 Project: Prosthetic Hand

October 13th and 17th
❮ ❯

For the Week 4 Project, I was introduced to a 3D-printed prosthetic hand. We have 3D printed the components of the open-source prosthetic hand and assembled them using a kit and some documentation manuals.
